01

Problem

Application data was fragmented across separate 2025 and 2026 datasets with no centralized reporting. Leadership had no real-time visibility into application volume, paid conversions, or month-wise growth — all tracked manually in Excel, causing data inconsistency and slow decision-making.

02

Approach

Appended multi-year datasets into a unified table using Power Query, standardized inconsistent program/branch data with TRIM + UPPER logic, and built a star-schema model (Fact: Applications · Dim: Calendar). Engineered advanced DAX measures for UTD same-date comparison, YoY Growth %, and Conversion Rate — with ISBLANK() guards to eliminate misleading -100% growth artifacts.

03
